Barn Owl Scientific Name. The barn owl was one of several species of bird first described in 1769 by the tyrolean physician and naturalist giovanni antonio scopoli in his anni historico naturales he gave it the scientific name strix alba. As more species of owl were described the genus name strix came to be used solely for the wood owls in the typical owl family strigidae and the barn owl became tyto alba in the barn.
